Books

Progettare i database - SQL E PHP

Camagni Paolo and Nikolassy Riccardo – How to design relational databases, use SQL to manage data, and connect databases to PHP for dynamic web applications.

Statistica: principi e metodi

Giuseppe Cicchitelli, Pierpaolo D'Urso and Marco Minozzo – Foundations of statistics, data analysis, probability, and statistical inference.

Probabilità e statistica per l'ingegneria e le scienze

Sheldon M. Ross – Core concepts of probability and applied statistical methods, from descriptive statistics and probability theory to inference and quality control.

Introduzione alla statistica

Luigi Pace & Alessandra Salvan – Introduction to statistical inference, likelihood-based methods and fundamental statistical models.

Inferenza statistica

Adelchi Azzalini – Statistical inference based on the likelihood principle, presenting core inferential methods and classical statistical theory.

Modello lineare. Teoria e applicazioni con R

Matteo Grigoletto, Laura Ventura & Francesco Pauli – Core introduction to linear regression models and their extensions, explaining how to model relationships between variables with practical examples using R.

Biostatistica. Casi di studio in R

Walter Racugno & Laura Ventura – Applied biostatistics for life sciences, teaching key statistical methods through real case studies.

An Introduction to Applied Multivariate Analysis with R

Brian Everitt & Torsten Hothorn – Mltivariate data analysis techniques using R, including visualization, principal components, clustering, factor analysis, and other methods.

Serie storiche economiche: analisi statistiche e applicazioni

Tommaso Di Fonzo & Fabio Lisi – Introduction to economic time series analysis, covering statistical methods for modeling, forecasting, and interpreting economic data.

Sampling: Design and Analysis

Sharon L. Lohr – Comprehensive guide to sampling methods, covering survey design, estimation techniques, and practical data analysis for accurate and efficient results.

Controllo statistico della qualità

Douglas C. Montgomery – Introduction to statistical quality control, covering process monitoring, control charts, and improvement techniques.

Information retrieval: metodi e modelli per i motori di ricerca

Massimo Melucci – Overview of information retrieval principles and models, explaining how search engines index, retrieve, and rank information using statistical and algorithmic techniques.

Deep learning

Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio & Aaron Courville – Comprehensive introduction to deep learning, covering neural networks, optimization, regularization, and practical applications in AI and data science.
